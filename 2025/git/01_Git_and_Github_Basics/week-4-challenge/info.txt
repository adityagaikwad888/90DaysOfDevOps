90 Days of DevOps is a structured learning challenge designed to help individuals gain hands-on experience 
and foundational knowledge in DevOps practices over a period of 90 days.

Each week focuses on a specific topic or tool, gradually building expertise and handson in areas such as:
- Version control
- CI/CD
- Automation
- Cloud platforms
- Containerization
- Monitoring
- And more

This challenge is ideal for beginners and professionals looking to strengthen their DevOps skills 
through consistent, practical learning.
